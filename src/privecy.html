We track activities on our Service and store certain information using cookies and other tracking technologies.

Cookies are little data files that may contain an anonymous unique identification.

Cookies are little text files that a website sends to your browser and stores there. Beacons, tags, and scripts are just a few of the tracking technologies that are used to gather data, track users, and analyze and enhance our service.

We use cookies for the following reasons:

Functional. Your experience will be customized using these cookies. For instance, cookies enable our Service to remember the information you have entered into forms so that you don't have to do so again and again.

Strictly necessary/Technical. These cookies are essential for us to be able to run our service and provide you with the access you've requested. Cookies fall under this category as well because they help us protect the Service and remember your browsing history during a given session.

Analytical/Performance. These cookies are used to analyze how the Service is used and how well it performs by SoftPartners Ltd. or a third-party service provider. For instance, they keep tabs on the most popular pages and the origin of visitors. For instance, they contain cookies from Google Analytics.

Most web browsers accept cookies by default. You are not required to accept cookies, and you can refuse or block their use as well as remove those already on your device. Click "help" in your browser's menu to learn how to accomplish this for your specific browser.
