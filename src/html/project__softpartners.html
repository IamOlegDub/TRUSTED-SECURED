3D study software

The software helps to use 3D anisotropy analysis approach will be used for manufacturing novel nanocomposites with tailored anisotropy.

The program helps to investigate the indicative optical effects in anisotropic crystals of all symmetry classes, as well as for calculating the increase in stability at the point of extreme.

management portal

SoftPartners have developed a website project management portal (PMP) that allows consortium members to communicate and share documents.

All documents, including project images, videos, graphic materials, and reports, are kept online in the PMP, allowing all project members to access them quickly.
